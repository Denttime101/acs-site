Bumper Repair — Common Questions

How much does bumper repair cost in Orange County?

Most ACS repairs come in well below a body shop — often under your deductible. The cost depends on damage severity. Call 949-282-9653 for a free estimate.

Can you repair a bumper cracked all the way through?

Yes. ACS repairs bumpers even if torn or punctured. The area is filled, reinforced, and refinished to factory spec with a full PPG color match.

How long does bumper repair take?

Most bumper repairs are completed in a few hours on-site at your Orange County home or office. No rental car, no shop drop-off.

Will the repair match the rest of my car?

Guaranteed. PPG's professional paint matching system identifies your factory color code and blends the repair seamlessly on any make or model.

Do you repair bumpers on Tesla and luxury vehicles?

Yes. Darren has dealer-level experience with BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Land Rover, Aston-Martin, and Tesla. One customer paid less than 25% of Tesla's quote for the same repair.

Is bumper repair better than replacement?

In most cases, yes — faster, cheaper, and preserves your original panel. ACS gives you an honest assessment and only recommends replacement when truly necessary.
