What Is Mobile Bumper Repair?

Mobile bumper repair is exactly what it sounds like — a professional auto cosmetic technician comes to your location with all the equipment needed to repair and refinish your bumper on-site. Auto Cosmetic Solutions uses PPG's professional paint system to sand, prime, fill, and repaint your bumper to factory spec. The result is a seamless color match that's guaranteed for as long as you own the vehicle.

ACS can repair bumpers even if they are torn or punctured — not just scratched. Owner Darren Burt has dealer-level experience with every major brand, including Tesla, BMW, Audi, Mercedes, Land Rover, and Aston-Martin. If the damage is cosmetic, we can fix it without a shop visit.

Bumper Repair — Common Questions

How much does bumper repair cost in Orange County?

Most ACS bumper repairs come in well below a traditional body shop — often under your insurance deductible. The exact cost depends on damage severity. Call 949-282-9653 for a free, no-obligation estimate.

Can you repair a bumper cracked all the way through?

Yes. ACS repairs bumpers even if they are torn or punctured. The area is professionally filled, reinforced, and refinished to factory spec with a full PPG color match.

How long does bumper repair take?

Most bumper repairs are completed in a few hours on-site at your home or office. You won't need a rental car or a shop drop-off. We work around your schedule.

Will the repair match the rest of my car?

Guaranteed. We use PPG's professional paint matching system to identify your factory color code and blend the repair seamlessly — on any make, model, or color.

Do you do bumper repair on Tesla and luxury vehicles?

Yes. Darren has dealer-level experience with BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Land Rover, Aston-Martin, and Tesla. One customer had their 2019 Tesla bumper repaired for less than 25% of what Tesla quoted.

Is bumper repair better than replacement?

In most cases, yes — repair is faster, cheaper, and preserves your original panel. ACS will always give you an honest assessment and only recommend replacement when the structural damage truly warrants it.
